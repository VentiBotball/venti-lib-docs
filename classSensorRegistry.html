Detailed Description

A class for managing sensors and their calibration data.

The SensorRegistry class is used to store and manage sensors (both analog and digital), as well as their associated calibration data. The class supports creating sensors, adding them to registries, and retrieving their values. It also handles internal sensors such as the accelerometer and gyroscope.

Detailed Description

Central registry for managing analog and digital sensors, including access to their calibration data.

Provides methods to create, add, and retrieve sensors by ID. Supports both unsigned short analog sensors and bool digital sensors.

Also allows associating each analog sensor with a CalibrationStore for retrieving named calibration values like "black" or "white".
